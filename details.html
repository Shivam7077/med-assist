About Us

Regency Specialist Hospital (Regency) is a licensed 218-bed private tertiary care hospital providing a comprehensive range of medical and surgical services. Strategically located within Iskandar Malaysia in the growing township of Bandar Seri Alam, Johor, Regency provides high quality healthcare to the local community and the regional countries. Established in November 2009, Regency is supported by more than 50 accredited local and internationally trained doctors as well as over 200 healthcare staff for its clinical expertise in cardiology, paediatrics, oncology, neurology, and with modern equipment to back its services. Regency is the first private hospital in Johor Bahru to be approved under Ministry of Health, Singapore, for the use of Medisave overseas for the approved hospitalisation and day surgeries. Regency was conferred the Malaysian Healthcare Travel Council (MHTC) certification by Ministry of Health, Malaysia, for providing healthcare services to regional patients. Regency is the sister hospital of Mahkota Medical Centre, a leading private tertiary care hospital in Malacca and leader in medical tourism in Malaysia. Both hospitals are members of Health Management International (HMI) Ltd, a listed healthcare company on the Singapore Exchange (SGX).
